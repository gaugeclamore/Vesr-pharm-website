What are my personal values?

  • Integrity
  • Respect
  • Empathy
  • Growth
  • Community

    • integrity

    You earn trust, make good decisions, and feel confident in doing what's right. You form integrity by consistently being honest, keeping your promises, and standing by your principles even when it's difficult.

    Respect

    You treat others with kindness, consideration, and appreciation. You show respect by listening to others, valuing their opinions, and acknowledging their feelings and rights.

    Empathy

    You understand and share the feelings of others. You practice empathy by putting yourself in someone else's shoes, being compassionate, and offering support when needed.

    Growth

    You strive to improve yourself and your abilities. You embrace growth by seeking new experiences, learning from your mistakes, and being open to feedback.

    Community

    You value being part of a group and contributing to the well-being of others. You foster community by participating in group activities, helping those in need, and building strong relationships.
